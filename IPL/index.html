About the Yellow Army

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, which plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team plays its home matches at the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai. The Super Kings have won the IPL title a record **five times** (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023), and the Champions League Twenty20 twice (2010, 2014).

Led by the legendary **Mahendra Singh Dhoni**, CSK is known for its consistent performance, strong team spirit, and a massive, passionate fan base often referred to as the "Yellow Army." Our motto, "Whistle Podu!", encapsulates the excitement and unwavering support of our fans.

We strive for excellence on the field and embody sportsmanship, making us one of the most respected and successful teams in IPL history. Join us in celebrating every victory and cheering for our Super Kings!
